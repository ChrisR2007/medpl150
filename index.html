Chris Ragoonath

I bring a twist to images and videos that grasp your attention. My work explores making moments stand out and special that wouldn't necessarily have your attention if I didn't interact with it. Inspired by comedic movies and short form content, I like to put both together to entertain. Most of my work is in video editing and slight photo editing. My work is about how any moment can be as good as you make it to be. I like to grow and try new things while upping the difficulty to be the best I can be.
